Become a Benefactor

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you), you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also receive two complimentary tickets to out next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at out annual Season's Launch function. Please complete the details below and mail to PO Box 576 Townsville 4810, make cheques payable to North Queensland Ensembles Inc. or you can scan and e-mail this to brotownsville@gmail.com and pay by direct debit to BSB 034 222 Account no. 173922. Donations are tax deductable. For more information, contact Simon McConnell (ph: 0418776961, e-mail: mcconnellgray@optusnet.com.au. Your support of the Orchestra will be greatly appreciated.
